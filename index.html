About

October University of Technology (OTU) is an Egyptian university affiliated with the Ministry of Education and Higher Education. It aims to produce a generation capable of keeping up with the labor market in all fields of work.

More Information

It is an Egyptian university with two colleges: a college of industry and energy and a college of health sciences. A college of industry and energy. It has many departments: a department of railways, information technology, textiles, food manufacturing, and a college of health sciences. It also has many departments, a department of pharmaceutical industries, dental implants, and medical information management.

College of industry and energy

Information Technology

It is a department that aims to produce a graduate capable of keeping up with the market. The student studies many specializations, including cybersecurity, networks, and software.

Railway department

It is a department specialized in railways and trains of all types and their operating and maintenance systems

Spinning and weaving department

It is a department specialized in spinning and weaving machines and how to manufacture and operate them

Food manufacturing department

It is a department specialized in food ingredients, the manufacture of canned goods, and other matters related to food and its quality

College of health sciences

Medical records department

Health information management is the process of inputting, managing, protecting, and analyzing health care data like electronic health records, lab results, patient contact information, medical history, and more. This information is highly-sensitive data that needs to be stored safely and securely while easily accessible and accurately located.

Dental Prosthodontics Department

A dental prosthesis consists of a product or a material that is used to replace a missing tooth or to cover up misshape or defective teeth. The dental prosthesis includes implants, crowns, and veneers. Some of these dental prostheses are removable while others are permanently attached.
